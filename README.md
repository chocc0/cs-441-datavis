## Notes Regarding Article Changes

We have made a few modifications to our original article since M2. Originally, our article used to mention the effects of pollution on mental health, however, we found little correlation within our dataset between the two. Instead, we found a potential correlation between graduation rates between various demographics and pollution rates, and thus we have inserted a section discussing that relationship and its implications.

For the visualizations, we modified the radar plots to display graduation rates split by race, rather than pollutants due to the lack of variety of pollutant types. The scatter plot was implemented in order to best display the relationship between air pollution and cardiovascular disease for a variety of cities. In the non-static visualizations, the scatterplot will be adjustable to allow users to hover and identify various cities displayed.
